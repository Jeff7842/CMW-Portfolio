Professional Profile

Professional Summary

Seasoned Commercial and Business Development Director with over 15 years of experience in renewable energy, power infrastructure, and clean tech market expansion across Sub-Saharan Africa. Specializes in franchise-style rollout models, C&I solar EPCs, IPP advisory, and large-scale project execution. Skilled in managing multi-site deployments, vendor networks, regulatory compliance, CRM-based pipeline tracking, and international stakeholder engagement.

Core Strengths & Experience

  • Franchise-style development using CODO, DOOO models for fuels and clean energy
  • End-to-end site activity, feasibility, permitting, contractor management, and launch execution
  • Clean energy consultancy: solar PV, BESS, corporate PPAs, and EPC structuring
  • CRM and data-driven commercial strategy with the World Bank, AfDB, and major DFIs
  • Governmental commercial strategy with international reporting to HQs in Geneva and China

Selected Achievements

  • Connected 14,000+ customers under Last Mile Connectivity via WB & MDB programs
  • Delivered $87M worth of mini-grid electrification projects
  • Executed solar + powerline EPCs for telecom and mining sectors
  • Closed multi-year PPA and fuel supply deals with industrial clients
